Real World Protection

Urban Commuting

Kavalr Response: Detects instability 0.3 seconds before the slide begins, deploying protective cushions around critical joints before impact. Integrated brake light flash alerts vehicles behind.

Result: Rider maintains control with minimal risk of injury, avoiding a potentially serious accident.

Highway Riding

Scenario: A vehicle suddenly changes lanes, forcing the rider to make an evasive maneuver at high speed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Kavalr detect accidents before they happen?

Kavalr uses a sophisticated neural network that analyzes motion patterns, traction data, and environmental factors at 500 times per second. By comparing real-time data against thousands of accident scenarios, our AI can predict potential accidents 0.3 seconds before they occur, giving the system crucial time to prepare protection systems.

Is installation complicated?

Not at all! Kavalr is designed for easy installation on any two-wheeler. The universal mounting system adapts to all motorcycle, scooter, and e-bike frames. Most customers complete installation in under 15 minutes with no special tools required. We also offer video guides and premium installation services for those who prefer professional help.

How does the self-charging technology work?

Kavalr features revolutionary kinetic energy harvesting technology that converts your bike's motion into electrical energy. As you ride, micro-generators capture energy from vibrations and movement, continuously charging the system. This means Kavalr never needs to be plugged in - it's always ready to protect you.

What happens after the protection deploys?

After deployment, Kavalr's protection cushions remain inflated for up to 4 hours to ensure ongoing protection. The system automatically notifies emergency services if needed and sends an alert to your designated contacts. Replacement cartridges are easily installed in seconds, getting you back on the road with full protection quickly.

Is my data secure with the connected features?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. All data is encrypted end-to-end, and we never sell or share your personal information. Location data is only transmitted in emergency situations or with your explicit permission. You have full control over what data is collected and how it's used.
